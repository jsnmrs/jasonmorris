--- layout: post title: Deck Dancing via Tripod fittext: 1.25 ---

While I'm usually content shooting photos and video of other people, sometimes I feel like capturing some of my own riding. Most times that means setting up the camera on a tripod and pressing record.

I gathered up some of those tripod clips and put together a quick video that my Least Most cohort Crandall persuaded me to post online. Not the most groundbreaking maneuvers, but fun to look back on regardless.

Looking back, I realized that in addition to the riding and filming, I also built every ramp you'll see in this video. Sometimes you have to take things into your own hands.
