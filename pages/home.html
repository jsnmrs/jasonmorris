Hi, I’m Jason

A surprising number of folks go by the same name. Their friends email me all the time.

I live in upstate New York with my talented significant other and our 7-year-old kiddo.

I’m a Certified Professional in Web Accessibility (CPWA) who works as an Accessibility Engineer and Lead for Development and Digital Strategy Teams at CommunicateHealth. I also tinker at doublegreat.dev. I typed my first bit of HTML in a Notepad window sometime in 1998 and have been writing code ever since.

Other platforms where I maintain a presence with varying degrees of participation: CodePen, GitHub, LinkedIn, Mastodon
